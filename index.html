CREATIVE BRIEF: GUJARAT TOURISM

What is the business opportunity/problem?

To reposition Gujarat from a family/religious destination to a vibrant, youthful choice for travelers (18-35), overcoming a fragmented brand identity and low recall in this demographic.

How will brand communication help?

Launch a campaign to shift perception from "traditional" to "vibrant & adventurous," establishing Gujarat as a land of endless celebration for young travelers.

GET (target; insight)

Young, experience-driven travelers who live and share their lives online.
Insight: I'm told travel should be about quiet escapes, but my soul craves energy, connection, and a vibrant celebration of life.

WHO (think / feel / do)

Think Gujarat is for their parentsâ€”a place for temples and heritage, not adventure or fun. They feel it lacks the "cool factor" and overlook it.

TO (think / feel / do)

Realise Gujarat is a hub of vibrant celebrations and unique adventures. Feel excited by its energy and book a trip to be part of it.

BY TELLING THEM (single-minded proposition)

Gujarat celebrates life in its truest form, inviting you to find your own rhythm in its endless festivities.

BECAUSE (reasons to believe)

Navratri, Rann Utsav, International Kite Festival, the only home of Asiatic Lions, and modern marvels like the Statue of Unity.

BRAND TONALITY

Your most energetic friend, inviting you to the greatest party you've ever seen.

MANDATORIES

  • Feature real travelers, not actors.
  • Focus on experiences, not just locations.
  • Modern, upbeat soundtrack.
  • Social-media-first aesthetic.
  • Campaign Hashtag: #ComeCelebrateWithUs

DELIVERABLES

A digital-first campaign including short-form videos (Reels/Shorts), influencer collaborations, a hero film (YouTube), and a User-Generated Content campaign.
